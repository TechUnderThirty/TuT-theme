About Tech Under Thirty

Tech Under Thirty is a budding group for technology-oriented people under the age of thirty. We bring designers, developers, engineers, and other people from the tech industry together to form friendships and cross-pollinate ideas.

Founded in 2013 by Catt Small, Tech Under Thirty hosts monthly meetings, workshops, and outings for people to learn new skills in a fun and friendly environment. Our goal is to build a community of confident, business-ready young people in tech fields.
