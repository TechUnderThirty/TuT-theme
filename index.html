--- layout: default title: Tech Under Thirty ---

Do something. Learn something. A meetup for design & tech-loving Millenials.

Tech Under Thirty is a new group for designers & tech-oriented people under the age of thirty. If you’re a game developer, a web designer, a print designer, an engineer, blogger, or anything in between, we’re interested in meeting you.

Tech Under Thirty’s purpose is to give young people in design and technology fields a chance to branch out amongst their peers. Students and those interested in getting into design & tech-related fields are welcome to join us. No experience required!
